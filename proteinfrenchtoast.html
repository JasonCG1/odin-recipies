Protein French Toast

Description

This wonderful dish can be served as a protein packed breakfast that will provide fuel for the entire day or as a delicious post workout snack. The protein toast yields a whopping 50 grams of protein per serving and will help anyone in their quest for gym gains.

Why not also turn this versatile dish into a dessert by topping it with syrup? Or how about whipped cream? The possibilites are endless.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Eggs and Egg whites
  • Milk
  • Protein powder
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla extract

Method

  1. Start by whisking together all the ingredients, except for the bread. Once combined, dip each slice of bread generously in the liquid mixture, ensuring all parts of it are coated.
  2. Next, add some cooking spray onto a non-stick pan and place it over medium heat.
  3. Once hot, add the soaked bread and cook for 6-7 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  4. Repeat the process until all the bread has been cooked.
  5. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve immediately.
